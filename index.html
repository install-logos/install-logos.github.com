Why Lo/g/os?

I have studied why the other OS developed by /g/ failed. Technically, most of them, if not all, were parochially successful with a few stable .iso releases but they failed to leave prints on the sands of time. >Lo/g/os cannot be another Jiyuu or Clover OS. These both failed because they had nothing new to offer. Lo/g/os, is a customizable Archlinux that can be installed like Ubuntu. You choose WM theme and choose Firefox theme and choose VIM theme and you are done

Lo/g/os offers a db full of custom themes: pre-configured packages installed with a simple command like pacman (rice vim-light or rice openbox-mlp).

What do we need to build?

  • A rice db / repo
  • A Package Manager for rice db (can also be used on other distros)

What Lo/g/os is?

  • A bloatware-free Arch distro with an installer and UI manager

Who is Lo/g/os for?

  • Anyone who needs a quick installation of Arch Linux
  • Anyone who needs a riced distro

How do you propose to achieve this?

  • The Arch Way: does exactly what it needs to, no extras, bloat, or choosing for the user
  • Any ricable config is uploadable
